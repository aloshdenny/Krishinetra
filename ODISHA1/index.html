How it works

Krishinetra is quite simple to use. Here, let me give you a tour:

1

Registration

Go to Registration on the dashboard and hit 'Sign Up' to create your account.
2

Set up your profile

Proceed to fill in details for your Krishinetra profile, your phone number and your residential address.
3

All good to go

The profile dashboard should display your location, soil profile, weather conditions and a wide array of saplings to choose from.
